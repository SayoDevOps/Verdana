Our Philosophy

Plants are

At VERDANA, we believe every plant tells a story. From the cloud forests of Ecuador to the highlands of Thailand, we travel the world to bring you specimens that cannot be found elsewhere.

Our botanists personally inspect every plant before it joins our collection. Each one ships with a care card, a botanical history, and a 30-day health guarantee.
