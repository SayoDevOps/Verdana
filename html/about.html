Our Origin

Where passion
meets botany

VERDANA was founded in 2018 by a small group of botanists, travellers, and plant obsessives who believed the world's most extraordinary plants deserved a platform worthy of their beauty.

What started as a small collection of rescued specimens has grown into a curated marketplace of over 340 species — each one sourced, inspected, and authenticated by our botanical team before reaching your home.

What We Stand For

Our values

Ethical Sourcing
Every plant is sourced from certified nurseries operating under CITES guidelines. We never wild-collect.
Plant Health First
Our in-house botanists inspect every specimen. Each plant ships with a 30-day health guarantee.
Lifetime Support
Your relationship with us doesn't end at checkout. Our botanists are available for care guidance forever.
